Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get to Taniti?

There are daily flights from major cities to Taniti's international airport.

What currency is used in Taniti?

Taniti uses the Tani Dollar. Most international currencies are accepted at local exchange booths.

How do I make payments for bookings?

Payments can be made via credit/debit card, PayPal, and bank transfer. We accept most major credit cards.

What is the refund policy?

If you cancel 30 days before your booking date, you'll receive a full refund. Cancellations made within 30 days will incur a 50% cancellation fee.
